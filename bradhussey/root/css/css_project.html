Cybersecurity is the protection of computer systems fom theft or damage to their hardware, software or electronic media, as well as from disruption or misdirection of the services they provide. The dark web is the WorldWide Web content that exists on darknets, overlay networks that use Internet but require specific software, configurations, or authorization to access.
